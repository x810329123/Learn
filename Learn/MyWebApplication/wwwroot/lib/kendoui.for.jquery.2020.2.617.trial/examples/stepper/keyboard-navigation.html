Keyboard legend

  • Alt + w focuses the widget
  • Shift + Tab or left arrow or up arrow focuses previous step
  • Tab or right arrow or down arrow focuses next step
  • home focuses first step
  • end focuses last step
  • Space or Enter selects focused step
  • To move the focus away from the Stepper:
    Focus the last step and press Tab or Focus the first step and press Shift + Tab

Select on focus

  • When Select on focus mode of the Stepper is enabled, the selection in the stepper will move together with the focus. Moving the focus to another step using the Tab or the arrow keys will also move the selection.
